About Us

Founded in the early 1980s in Spain, Ph Charol began as a pioneer in medical skincare, specializing in creams designed to treat itching, burning, and various skin conditions. Our commitment to quality and innovation quickly established us as a trusted name in the skincare industry.

As our product line expanded, so did our reach. In 2000, we opened a new branch in China, in partnership with Hong Kong International Marketing Group Ltd., allowing us to serve a global market and continue our mission of providing effective skincare solutions to millions worldwide.
